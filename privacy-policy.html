Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Effective Date: August 7, 2025
Last Updated: August 7, 2025

Information We Collect

Location Data: CleanPlate may access your device's location to help you find nearby restaurants. This data is used only for restaurant search functionality and is not stored or shared with third parties.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance, including search queries and feature usage patterns. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

No Personal Information: CleanPlate does not collect, store, or require any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

How We Use Information

  • Restaurant Search: Location data helps provide relevant restaurant results in your area
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us enhance features and performance
  • Service Delivery: Data is used solely to provide restaurant health inspection information
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with our app to make improvements

Data Sources

CleanPlate displays publicly available health inspection data from official city health departments including:

  • New York City Department of Health and Mental Hygiene
  • Chicago Department of Public Health
  • Boston Public Health Commission
  • Austin Public Health
  • Seattle & King County Public Health

All health inspection data is sourced from official government databases and is publicly available information.

Third-Party Services

Analytics: We use anonymous analytics services to understand app usage patterns. No personal data is included in these analytics reports.

External Links: CleanPlate may contain links to restaurant websites or other external services. When you click these links, you may be subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with these services.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Location data is processed locally on your device for restaurant search purposes and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Data Security

CleanPlate implements appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect any data processed by the app. Since we minimize data collection, there is minimal personal data at risk.

All data transmission occurs over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) to ensure your information remains secure.

Children's Privacy

CleanPlate is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Location Access: You can enable or disable location access in your device settings at any time
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't store personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to delete
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the app at any time to cease all data collection
  • Contact Us: You can contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

International Users

CleanPlate is primarily designed for users in the United States, where our restaurant data sources are located. If you use our app from outside the U.S., please be aware that information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Notify users through the app if changes are material

Your continued use of CleanPlate after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store privacy requirements
  • Apple App Store privacy guidelines
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
