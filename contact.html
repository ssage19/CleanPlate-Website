Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I report incorrect restaurant information?
If you notice incorrect health inspection data, please email us at ssage@cleanplateus.com with the restaurant name, location, and specific details about the discrepancy. We source our data from official health departments, so we'll investigate and coordinate with the appropriate authorities.
‚ùì Can you add my city to CleanPlate?
We're always looking to expand to new cities! Email ssage@cleanplateus.com with your city name and we'll investigate the availability of health inspection data from your local health department.
‚ùì Do you accept restaurant advertising or sponsorships?
To maintain transparency and trust, CleanPlate does not accept restaurant advertising or paid sponsorships. We do have affiliate partnerships with food delivery services, which are clearly disclosed. For other partnership inquiries, contact ssage@cleanplateus.com.
‚ùì How often is restaurant data updated?
We sync with health department databases regularly, typically every 24-48 hours. However, the frequency of updates depends on how often each health department publishes new inspection data. Some cities update daily, others weekly or monthly.
