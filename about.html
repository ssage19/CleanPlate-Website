About CleanPlate

Making dining decisions safer, one inspection at a time

CleanPlate was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves to know the health inspection status of restaurants before they dine. We believe transparency in food safety creates better experiences for diners and encourages restaurants to maintain the highest standards.

üåü Our Values

Transparency: We believe in open access to public health information and clear communication about our data sources and methodologies.

Accuracy: We are committed to providing reliable, up-to-date information sourced directly from official government databases.

Privacy: We respect user privacy and minimize data collection while providing maximum value to our users.
