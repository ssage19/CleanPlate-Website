üî¨ Data Processing & Quality Assurance

Automated Sync: CleanPlate connects directly to each city's health department data systems. Our systems automatically retrieve new inspection data every 24 hours to ensure freshness.

Data Validation: Every inspection record goes through automated validation checks to ensure data integrity. Any anomalies are flagged for manual review.

Data Standardization: Since each city uses different grading systems and violation codes, we standardize the data presentation while preserving the original information. This allows users to easily compare restaurants across different cities.

üîÑ Update Frequency & Reliability

Sync Schedule: CleanPlate automatically checks for new inspection data every 24 hours. However, the frequency of new data depends on how often each health department publishes updates:

  • Daily Updates: NYC, Chicago, Austin, Seattle (high-volume cities with frequent inspections)
  • Weekly Updates: Boston (regular publishing schedule)

Data Freshness: The "Last Updated" timestamp shown in the app reflects when each city's health department last published new data, not when CleanPlate retrieved it. This ensures transparency about data currency.

Reliability Monitoring: We continuously monitor our data sources for availability and consistency. If a source becomes unavailable, users are notified within the app, and we work to restore the connection as quickly as possible.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Disclaimers

Data Accuracy: While we strive for accuracy, CleanPlate is not responsible for errors in the original government data sources. Users should verify current restaurant conditions independently.

Inspection Timing: Health inspection data may not reflect the most recent restaurant conditions. Inspections occur at different intervals, and conditions can change between visits.

Data Completeness: Not all restaurants in covered cities may appear in CleanPlate if they haven't been inspected recently or if they're not included in the public datasets.

Third-Party Links: Links to official government websites are provided for transparency and verification. CleanPlate is not responsible for the content or availability of these external sites.
